Hello, World! I'm Claire Roca

From a young age, I've had a knack for computers, and I began my journey into my computer science career in high school when I took my first programming class and discovered my passion. I'm an aspiring software developer with a special focus on web development.

I graduated as my high school's valedictorian and completed my computer science degree in only three years. Throughout my academic journey, I worked as a computer science tutor, helping other students grasp complex concepts and improve their programming skills.

When I'm not coding, I can be found either spending time with my family, drawing, gaming, or playing Dungeons & Dragons. These hobbies not only help me relax but also inspire creativity and strategic thinking, which I bring back to my development work.

My passion for programming stems from being able to apply what I've learned in a practical way with a visual result. My current personal project is a D&D character creation site where I can apply all I've learned from my courses and Capstone Project. This project allows me to combine my love for ttrpgs with my web development skills, creating a tool that enhances the gaming experience for myself and others.
